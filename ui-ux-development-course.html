This UI/UX design course helps you learn the complete product design process used in modern digital companies. The program covers UX research, user journey mapping, information architecture, wireframing, UI design, and interactive prototyping. Students learn how real digital products are designed from idea to interface using modern UX methodologies.
During this UX design training, you will work with industry tools such as Figma and FigJam to design dashboards, business systems, and real product interfaces. The course focuses on practical learning through exercises, design challenges, and real-world case studies so students understand how UX designers solve real user problems.
The program also introduces product thinking, data-driven design, usability testing, and design systems used in professional UX workflows. By working on real projects and building a complete UX case study, students develop a strong portfolio that demonstrates their UX design skills.
By the end of this UX design course, learners will understand how to research users, design intuitive interfaces, create interactive prototypes, and present professional UX case studies. This training prepares students for careers as UX designers, UI designers, product designers, and UX researchers in modern digital product teams.
